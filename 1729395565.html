【Campus Screening Project】Beyond Beauty - Taiwan from Above 小小齊柏林·看見台灣校園公播計畫-從高空看見我們的家園

2024/10/20 11:39 ·
活動公告 ·
永續發展中心 info 
Dear all,

The Climate Action Club of National Dong Hwa University (NDHU) and the Center for Sustainable Development, NDHU, cordially invite you to participate in the Campus Screening Project: Beyond Beauty – Taiwan from Above on Thursday, October 24, 2024, from 18:30 to 21:30 at Lecture Hall B119, College of Environmental Studies and Oceanography.

This event will take you on a visual journey exploring Taiwan’s stunning landscapes from the sky while addressing the environmental challenges the island faces. Following the screening, we will host a post-film discussion, conducted entirely in English and led by Professor Hsing-Sheng Tai from the Department of Natural Resources and Environmental Studies, where we will delve into Taiwan’s environmental issues and future strategies.

Event Schedule:
18:20 – 18:30: Check-in and entry
18:30 – 18:50: Event introduction
18:50 – 20:30: Film screening
20:30 – 21:30: Post-film discussion and group photo
Organizer: Chi Po-Lin Foundation and Climate Action Club, NDHU 
Co-organizer: Center for Sustainable Development, NDHU
Event Language: English & Chinese

Event Objectives:
1. Raise Awareness of Environmental and Climate Change: Explore the beauty of Taiwan’s natural environment and the impacts of climate change while discussing land development and sustainable growth.
2. Promote Climate Action on Campus: Inspire faculty and students to engage in sustainability initiatives and advocate for eco-friendly lifestyles.
3. Create a Platform for Knowledge Sharing: The post-film discussion offers diverse perspectives, fostering a deeper connection between international students and Taiwanese culture.

We warmly welcome all faculty, students, and members of the public to join us in working toward environmental protection and sustainable development! If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact the event coordinator.

We look forward to your participation!

大家好，
國立東華大學氣候行動社與永續發展中心誠摯邀請您參加，2024年10月24日（星期四）18:30-21:30 在環境暨海洋學院大樓B119講堂舉辦的【從高空看見我們的家園】校園公播計畫 - 放映《看見台灣》這部深具啟發性的紀錄片。

這場活動不僅將帶領大家從高空探索臺灣的壯麗自然景觀，也將探討臺灣面臨的環境挑戰。放映結束後，特別邀請國立東華大學環境暨海洋學院自然資源與環境學系的戴興盛教授進行全英文的映後座談，與大家深入討論臺灣環境問題及未來對策。

活動流程：
	•	18:20 - 18:30: 報到與進場
	•	18:30 - 18:50: 活動介紹
	•	18:50 - 20:30: 影片放映
	•	20:30 - 21:30: 映後座談與大合照

活動目標：
1.提高對環境與氣候變遷的認識：探索台灣的自然美景及氣候變遷的影響，並參與土地開發與永續發展的討論。
2.推動校園內的氣候行動：激勵校內師生參與永續發展倡議，並倡導綠色生活方式。
3.創造知識分享的平台： 映後座談提供多元觀點交流機會，促進外籍生與台灣文化的深度連結。

主辦單位：看見齊柏林基金會、國立東華大學氣候行動社
協辦單位：國立東華大學永續發展中心
活動語言：英文 & 中文

歡迎全校師生與各界人士踴躍參加，共同為環境保護與永續發展努力！
若有任何問題，請聯繫活動負責人。
