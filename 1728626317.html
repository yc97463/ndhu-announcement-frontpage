【K書中心公告】K中A館開放時間說明(第三次公告) 

2024/10/11 13:58 ·
其他公告 ·
教學卓越中心 info 
一、K書中心A館討論區短暫開放至2024年11月10日(日) 22:00
考量到同學們對學習空間的需求，教學卓越中心規劃暫時開放使用K書中心A館內損毀較小的討論區。K書中心A館討論區將自2024年9月23日（一）18:00起暫時開放至11月10日（日）22:00，具體開放時段請參見附圖。

二、放置在K中A、B兩館的個人物品，請統一於2024年10月31日(四)前至K中A館領取
目前K中A、B館仍有部分個人物品遺留在館內，這些物品已統一放置在K書中心A館。
請於K中A館開放時間內，攜帶學生證前來領取；如需代領，請代領人一併攜帶學生證，以便現場工作人員核對身份。
最遲須於2024年10月31日（四）22:00前完成。逾期未領取者，K書中心不負保管責任，並將統一清除遺留物品，敬請見諒。

教學卓越中心　敬啟
-----
Dear Faculty and Students,

K-Book Center A Discussion Area will be temporarily open until November 10, 2024 (Sunday) at 10:00 PM
To accommodate students', need for study spaces, the Center for Teaching Excellence has arranged to temporarily open the discussion area of K-Book Center A, which sustained minimal damage. The discussion area will be open from 6:00 PM on Monday, September 23, 2024, until 10:00 PM on Sunday, November 10, 2024. Please refer to the attached schedule for specific opening hours.

Please collect your personal items from K-Book Center A and B by Thursday, October 31, 2024
There are still some personal items left in K-Book Centers A and B, and they have been relocated to K-Book Center A for collection.
Please bring your student ID to collect your belongings during the open hours of K-Book Center A. If someone else is collecting on your behalf, they must also bring your student ID for verification by the staff.
The final deadline for collection is 10:00 PM on Thursday, October 31, 2024. After this deadline, K-Book Center will not assume responsibility for any remaining items, which will be disposed of.

Thank you for your understanding.

Sincerely,
Center for Teaching Excellence
