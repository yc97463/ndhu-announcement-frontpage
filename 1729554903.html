Bonjour! Are you trying to learn French and want to know more about French culture? Here's where you should look!

This semester, Language Center presents 10 sessions of French Lunch Hour for those who would like to learn more about French customs, language, cultures and traditions!

Join our French Lunch Hour every Thursday and have fun with our French teaching assistant Coleen!

(The sessions will be mainly conducted in Chinese and English, along with French.)

※Sign-up online and walk-in participation are both welcome!
※One hour of the cross-disciplinary self-learning credits
