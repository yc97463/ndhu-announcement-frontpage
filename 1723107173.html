Hi International Students of Dong Hwa University!

Ever thought about starting a business in Taiwan but don’t know where to begin? 
Don’t miss this invaluable sharing session with successful foreign entrepreneurs in Taiwan!

On the first day of the 2024 Meet Pacific Startups Festival, 9/27 (Fri.), we have:

🔸 Elias Ek
A pro with 24 years of entrepreneurial experience, co-founder of Enspyre, author of "How to Start a Business in Taiwan," and co-organizer of Dragons’ Chamber (Taiwan’s only pitch event for immigrant entrepreneurs). An Indian entrepreneur who studied in Hualien, co-founder of Pirate Coffee, and a graduate of Tzu Chi University’s Institute of Medical Sciences. He will share his journey and challenges in starting a business in Taiwan.
