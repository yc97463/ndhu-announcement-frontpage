【重要通知】9月27日 開放領取K書中心兩館遺留物品，請同學盡速前往K書中心A館領取

2024/09/27 09:40 ·
其他公告 ·
教學卓越中心 info

因0403、0423兩次地震影響，多數學生遺留個人物品於K書中心AB兩館，目前所有物品皆暫時放置於K書中心A館，本館不負物品保管責任，請同學盡速於今日前往K書中心A館領取。
為方便同學領取個人物品，請依照以下時間安排前往：
🔸K中A館領取時間：9月27日(五)上午9:00-12:00 或 下午14:00-17:30，攜帶學生證至K書中心A館現場辦理領取手續。
🔸若無法配合今日領取時間者，請於K書中心A館開放時間（開放時間請參考副件），最遲須於2024年10月31日（四）22:00前完成。
🔸敬請同學備妥學生證，以便現場工作人員核對身份。
🔸如需代領，請代領人一併攜帶學生證，以便現場工作人員核對身份。
🔸逾期未領取者，K書中心不負保管責任，並將統一清除遺留物品，敬請見諒。

感謝您的配合！

Due to the impact of the earthquakes on April 3 and April 23, many students left personal belongings in K-Book Centers A and B. All unclaimed items have now been temporarily relocated to K-Book Center A. Please note that the center is not responsible for safeguarding these items, and students are urged to collect their belongings as soon as possible.

For your convenience, please follow the collection schedule below:

K-Book Center A Collection Hours:

  • Date: Friday, September 27
  • Morning: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Afternoon: 2:00 PM - 5:30 PM
  • Location: K-Book Center A
    Please bring your student ID for identity verification when collecting your items.

🔸 If you cannot collect your items today, please visit K-Book Center A during its regular opening hours (refer to the attached document for details) and complete the collection by no later than 10:00 PM on Thursday, October 31, 2024.

🔸 Please have your student ID ready for verification by the staff.

🔸 If someone else is collecting on your behalf, they must also bring your student ID for verification by the staff.

🔸 Items not collected by the deadline will be disposed of, and K-Book Center will not assume responsibility for their safekeeping.

Thank you for your cooperation!
