【國際交流活動】Universitas Negeri Malang Vocational Inter-Cultural Camp 2024

2024/10/18 16:53 ·
活動公告 ·
國際事務處國際學生組 info 
English version in below

◎時間：2024年11月5日 – 2024年11月8日

◎地點：印尼 瑪琅州立大學

◎費用：機票、保險、交通費(行程表以外的交通費)、簽證(到印尼需簽證的國家才需要)及其他個人花費。

◎報名截止時間：2024.10.29

◎報名流程

1.填寫google 表單向國際處報名，同時繳交以下文件：

Google form：https://reurl.cc/2jAzQ9

*在學證明
*家長同意書

2.自行到姊妹校提供之連結報名: https://bit.ly/VICC2024Register

3.等待姐妹校通知。

其他相關資訊請參考宣傳簡章。

注意事項：

1.請務必詳細閱讀簡章後再報名。
2.國際處僅公告或協助本校學生報名參加簽約校的短期活動或課程，其短期課程是否可抵免本校學分，參加學生需遵照教務處相關規定辦理。若活動日期為本校學期上課期間，學生須依照本校規定自行辦理請假手續。

English version

◎Period： November 05 ~ November 08 , 2024

◎Location：Universitas Negeri Malang, Indonesia

◎Fee：Airfare, traveler’s insurance, transportation(arranged out of the designated schedule), Visa(when required for non no-visa country to enter Indonesia), other personal expenses during the program.

◎Registration Deadline：2024.10.29

1.Sign up via google form and upload below documents to OIA.

Google form：https://reurl.cc/2jAzQ9

*Student Status Certificate
*Parental Consent Letter

2. Complete application at : https://bit.ly/VICC2024Register

3. Await the announcement from Universitas Negeri Malang.

For further information, please visit their website and check out their brochure.

Please note:

1. Please mke sure to read the program brochure before applying.
2. The OIA only announces or assists our school’s students in registering for short-term activities or courses offered by contracted schools. Whether credits from these short-term courses can be transferred to our school is subject to the regulations of the Academic Affairs Office, and participating students must comply accordingly. If the activity falls within our school’s academic semester, students must follow our school’s regulations to apply for leave of absence.
