#永續發展中心籌備處【活動轉知】綠色和平帶你構想一場「反漂綠」倡議 工作坊 | Tips and Tricks to Develop an Anti-Greenwashing Campaign: Ideation Workshop

2024/04/22 16:59 · 研討會 

Care about climate change, but don’t know what you can do? 
Want to hold corporations responsible but doubt the power of an individual?
Want to start a social impact campaign but don’t know how to start? 
This workshop is for you!

關心氣候變遷，但不知道自己能做些什麼？
想要企業為碳排負責，卻不知以個人的力量如何監督？
想要為正義發聲，但不知道如何開始？
來我們的工作坊吧！

Reformosa has invited Greenpeace, an international NGO advocating for environmental protection since 1971, to share its experience and expertise and guide us in ideating campaigns to keep Taiwanese companies accountable for their carbon emissions and environmental impact. 

Reformosa福爾摩擦邀請了國際環保倡議組織綠色和平與我們分享他們在環保倡議超過50年的經驗與專業知識，並且通過此次工作坊指導我們構想反漂綠倡議，以監督台灣企業的碳排以及環境影響。
