◎地點：日本 芝浦工業大學 大宮校區

◎費用：機票、旅遊平安險、餐費、交通費、住宿費以及其他個人費用。

◎報名截止時間：2024.10.17

◎報名流程

國際處僅公告或協助本校學生報名參加簽約校的短期活動或課程，其短期課程是否可抵免本校學分，參加學生需遵照教務處相關規定辦理。若活動日期為本校學期上課期間，學生須依照本校規定自行辦理請假手續。

◎Period： December 10 – December 19, 2024

◎Location：Omiya campus of Shibaura Institute of Technology (SIT), Japan

◎Fee：Airfare, travel insurance, accommodation payment, daily transportation, food, other personal expenses during the program.

◎Registration Deadline：2024.10.17

1.Sign up via google form and upload below documents to OIA.

Google form：https://forms.gle/WmHWBvNoCszJfmY28

Student Status Certificate
Parental Consent Letter(Form：Parental Consent Letter)

2.If you want to apply, please request the application link from the Office of International Affairs Division of International Students, Doris Lu.

Email : issa@gms.ndhu.edu.tw

For further information, please visit their website and check out their brochure.

Please note:

The OIA only announces or assists our school’s students in registering for short-term activities or courses offered by contracted schools. Whether credits from these short-term courses can be transferred to our school is subject to the regulations of the Academic Affairs Office, and participating students must comply accordingly. If the activity falls within our school’s academic semester, students must follow our school’s regulations to apply for leave of absence.
