Feeling at Home in Taiwan: Empower Yourself with Legal Knowledge!

Calling all NDHU international students!

Feeling a little lost navigating the legalities of life in Taiwan? We've got you covered!
Join us for an insightful speech on Thursday, July 18th, from 13:30 to 15:30 titled: "Navigating Taiwan: Legal Essentials for International Students."
Our esteemed speaker, Prof. Dr. Bernard Y. Kao, a renowned legal expert and former Program Director of the Tricontinental Master Program in Global Studies (TRIM) at National Chung Hsing University, will be sharing his vast knowledge to equip you with the legal essentials you need to feel confident and secure in your Taiwanese experience.
Interested in what TRIM is and how much Prof. Dr. Kao has contributed to international academic and cultural exchanges?  Read this: What is TRIM? (https://shorturl.at/OmzNU)
Here's what you'll gain from this informative session:
Essential legal knowledge: Gain a clear understanding of key legal concepts relevant to your daily life in Taiwan.
Empowerment and Confidence: This session will empower you to navigate legal situations with confidence and peace of mind.
Ask the Expert: Prof. Dr. Kao will be available to answer your questions and address any specific concerns you may have.

Don't miss this valuable opportunity to gain valuable legal knowledge and feel more at home in Taiwan!
Register now and secure your spot! 
See you there!
