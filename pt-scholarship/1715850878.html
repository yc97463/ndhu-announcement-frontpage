#國際事務處國際學生組Internship for PhD students - Scholarship offer

2024/05/16 17:14 · 獎助學金 

Fahrenheit Universities would like to invite the best three International PhD students for 12-months research stay at one of the Fahrenheit Universities, i.e. University of Gdansk (https://en.ug.edu.pl/) , Gdansk University of Technology (https://pg.edu.pl/en), and Medical University of Gdansk (https://mug.edu.pl/).  

The participants of Fahrenheit Scholarships in a one-year internship (begins on January 2025) will receive a scholarship paid monthly (6500 PLN per months), free accommodation (provided by City of Gdańsk) and the reimbursement of travel costs.  
They encourage young scientists who are preparing a doctoral dissertation at your university, are the authors or joint authors of a scientific publication and wish to pursue their scientific interests at one of Polish leading universities - to take advantage of the offer. 
The deadline for submission of applications is June 15 this year. Details are attached and also available at website page

This is a unique opportunity not only to conduct interdisciplinary research as part of a PhD dissertation, but also to gain international experience and make new valuable contacts. 

In case of any questions or further information please contact the FarU Office at +48 511 946 646 or office@faru.edu.pl.
