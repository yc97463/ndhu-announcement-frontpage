2025第12屆「華山論見」教育學術研討會
大會主題：「潛力大躍進－AI時代的教學實務與潛能激發」
【研討會徵稿領域】
本次研討會的徵稿重點包括（但不限於）以下：
1.探討國內外教育實務上應用AI技術於各項教學實務的最新進展。 
2.分析與檢視AI應用對於素養教學的多層次影響。 
3.探討如何利用AI技術開發學生的潛能，激發他們的創造力和批判性思維。
4.AI時代如何增進職前與在職教師的相關知能，使其促進學生學習。
5.評估AI應用對教育研究與教育政策、課程教學等各層面的影響。
6.其他教育制度政策、課程教學等相關研究主題。
～我們誠摯歡迎投稿並分享給有興趣的朋友～
【活動形式】
1.大會專題演講
2.主題論壇
3.論文發表：口頭發表與「互動式」壁報發表

The 12th "Huashan Educational Seminar" will be held with the theme of "Great Leap in Potential – Teaching Practices and Potential Development in the AI Era."
【Submission Areas for Seminar】
The thematic areas for submission include (but are not limited to) the following:
1.	Exploring the latest developments in the application of AI technology in various educational practices domestically and internationally.
2.	Analyzing and examining the multi-level impacts of AI applications on competency-based teaching.
3.	Discussing how AI technology can be used to develop students' potential and stimulate their creativity and critical thinking.
4.	Enhancing pre-service and in-service teachers' relevant competencies in the AI era to better support student learning.
5.	Assessing the influence of AI applications on educational research, educational policies, curriculum design, and instruction.
6.	Other research topics related to educational policies, curriculum, and instruction.
～We sincerely welcome submissions and encourage you to share this news with the friends who may be interested.～
