(一)	演講主題
泰國教育趨勢與爭議
(二) 演講資訊
泰國農業大學(College of Education, Kasetsart University)教育學院院長Udomluk Koolsriroj教授，長期致力於教育的多樣性和公平性，並關注性別平等教育於泰國教育現場的發展與趨勢。
Udomluk Koolsriroj教授深耕於教學領導力以及課程與教學發展領域的同時，也具備13 年的英語教學經驗，Udomluk Koolsriroj教授將於本次專題演講中分享於泰國教學實務現場中所觀察到的泰國教育趨勢與爭議，期盼透過泰國的經驗與台灣的教育工作者進行對話與交流。

3.	Presentation Information
Prof. Udomluk Koolsriroj is an Assistant Professor and currently serves as the Dean of the Faculty of Education at Kasetsart University. 

Her academic expertise encompasses courses such as Diversity and Equity in Education, Leadership in Curriculum and Instruction, and Curriculum and Instruction Development. Prior to her tenure at Kasetsart University, Dr. Koolsriroj accumulated 13 years of experience teaching English at the University Laboratory School, Center for Educational Research and Development. She holds a Bachelor of Arts from Chulalongkorn University and both a Master of Arts in Applied Linguistics and a Doctor of Education in Curriculum and Instruction from Kasetsart University.

In this presentation, Prof. Udomluk Koolsriroj will share educational trends and controversies observed in the field of teaching practice in Thailand and look forward to dialoguing and exchanging experiences with educators in Taiwan.
