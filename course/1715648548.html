#資訊工程學系[CSIE] 2024 Summer Calculus I Course Survey

2024/05/14 09:02 · 課程公告 

To better understand your need and decide whether to open a Summer Course - Calculus (I) in English, the CSIE Department would love to know how many of you want to participate in it, if there is one. That is to say, the number of participants will be the key to opening it. Please complete the survey by 12:00 on 5/16 (Thu). Registration information will be announced right after the Survey Deadline or when the number of participants reaches the minimum requirements, i.e., 25 students.
  
Calculus I Summer Credit Course (Temporary Information, needs to be confirmed)
Date:   July 01-July 26, 2024
Time:   9:00-12:00 every weekday
Place:   C303 in Science Building II
Lecturer:  Yao Huaijie
Credit:  3
Fee:       3300/ each person
Survey Deadline: 113/05/16 (Thu) 12:00
Survey Link: https://forms.gle/ckowA3tQDfZZW7AUA

*The 3-credit Calculus (I) Course is planned to be a physical one open in July 2024 (basically 9:00-12:00 during weekdays, 18 sessions/54 hours in total, while the details still need to be confirmed) if the number of participants expresses the need for it.

*The course fee, NT$3,300, will be paid by yourself. 

* The attached syllabus is for your reference.  

If there is any problem, please don't hesitate to let us know. Thanks!
