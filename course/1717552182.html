#教務處課務組【課務組】112-2學期碩博士學位考試申請期限延至113年7月31日截止The application deadline for the degree examination for master's and doctoral graduates in the 112-2 semester of our school has been extended to July 31, 2024

2024/06/05 09:49 · 課程公告

【重要通知】本校112-2學期碩博士應屆畢業生學位考試申請期限延至113年7月31日截止，學位考試成績登錄延長至113年8月30日前完成。

一、為協助同學克服地震帶來的困難並順利取得學位，依據本校學則、專科以上學校維護突遭重大災害學生學習權益處理原則暨113.05.30簽案辦理。

二、本學期碩博士應屆畢業生之學位考試申請截止期限原訂113年6月7日，配合學位論文上傳及離校時間，延長至113年7月31日(星期三)截止。
    另學位考試成績送教務處註冊組登錄延至113年8月30日(星期五)前完成。

三、如有任何問題，請洽教務處以下單位：
    學位考試申請：課務組(聯絡電話:03-8906122~6)
    成績登錄：註冊組（聯絡電話:03-8906112~6）

 

 

[Important Notice] :The application deadline for the degree examination for master's and doctoral graduates in the 112-2 semester of our school has been extended to July 31, 2024, and the registration of degree examination results has been extended to be completed before August 30, 2024.

 

  1. In order to help students overcome the difficulties caused by the earthquake and successfully obtain their degrees, the case will be handled in accordance with NDHU Academic Regulations, the Principles for Protecting the Learning Rights of Students Affected by Major Disasters in Colleges and Above, and the Approval which signed on May 30, 2024.

  2. The application deadline for the degree examination for master's and doctoral graduates this semester was originally scheduled for June 7, 2024. It is planned to be extended to July 31, 2024 (Wednesday) coordinating with the uploading of dissertations and school- departure time. In addition, and the registration of degree examination results has been extended to be completed before August 30, 2024.

  3. If you have any questions, please contact the following units in the Academic Affairs Office:
    Degree examination application: Curriculum Division (contact number:03-8906122~6)
    Registration of degree examination results: Registry Division (contact number:03-8906112~6）
